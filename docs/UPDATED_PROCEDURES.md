# FCM Updated VFR Procedures

This document summarizes the updated VFR arrival and departure procedures at Flying Cloud Airport (FCM), including the new feeder/inside fix system and dual tower frequencies.

## Tower Frequencies

FCM now operates two tower frequencies:

| Frequency  | Use                                                          |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **125.2**  | North parallel runway operations and arrivals from the north |
| **119.15** | South parallel runway operations and arrivals from the south |

- When runway 18/36 is active, use **119.15**.
- When the tower is closed, use **119.15** as CTAF.
- The tower monitors both frequencies at all times. If arriving from the north, contact on **125.2** regardless of which runway is active.
- The ATIS will include the new frequency and arrival instructions.

## Ground Procedures

Request taxi as normal. Before arriving at the runway, expect either a "Monitor" or "Contact" instruction from ATC.
- No reply required on ground.
- **Monitor tower**: switch to the given tower frequency and expect tower to call you.
- **Contact tower**: switch to the given tower frequency and announce.
- Runway crossings are expected to be done on tower.
- **Never** enter a runway for any reason without explicit clearance.

## Departure Procedures

All VFR departures leaving FCM Class D airspace:

1. Fly extended runway centerline.
   - VFR departures should not receive "fly runway heading" instructions from tower
   - But if you do, interpret as "fly extended runway centerline"
2. Climb to **2,500' MSL**.
3. Tower will advise when to turn on course.

## Arrival Procedures

FCM-based flight school traffic is required to use the feeder fix and inside fix system.

Non-flight school traffic will opt-in to the new procedure by announcing their feeder fix on initial contact with tower. If no feeder fix is announced, tower will handle sequencing and pattern entry as normal.

### Overview

1. Contact tower on the appropriate frequency as "inbound" or "over" a **feeder fix**.
    - Tower reply of "roger" does not require acknowledgement, but indicates that you should continue to the paired inside fix for sequencing.
    - Do not enter Class D until establishing two-way communication with tower--you must hear your callsign.
2. Fly from the feeder fix to its paired **inside fix**, entering Class D at or below **2,000' MSL**.
3. If tower has not issued pattern entry instructions by the inside fix, report over the inside fix.
   - If tower _has_ issued pattern entry instructions, do not report over the inside fix.
4. Tower will issue pattern entry from the inside fix, or instruct you to _remain outside the downwind_.

### Feeder Fixes

Feeder fixes are the outer reporting points, approximately 7–9 NM from the airport. Arrive at or below the listed altitude.

| Fix             | Direction | Altitude     | Paired Inside Fix |
| --------------- | --------- | ------------ | ----------------- |
| **Island**      | NW        | 2,500' MSL   | Chanhassen        |
| **High School** | W         | 2,500' MSL   | Chanhassen        |
| **494/394**     | N         | 2,500' MSL   | Interchange       |
| **Soccer Dome** | SE        | _2,300' MSL_ | Bridge            |
| **Pit**         | SW        | 2,500' MSL   | Elevator          |
| **Prior Lake**  | S         | 2,500' MSL   | Elevator          |

Soccer Dome has a lower ceiling (2,300') due to the Class B shelf above it.

### Inside Fixes

Inside fixes are the inner reporting points, approximately 2.5–3.5 NM from the airport. You should be at or below 2,000' MSL before reaching the inside fix.

| Fix             | Direction |
| --------------- | --------- |
| **Chanhassen**  | NW        |
| **Interchange** | N         |
| **Bridge**      | SE        |
| **Elevator**    | SW        |

### Outside the Downwind

When traffic volume is high, tower may instruct you to **remain outside the downwind**. This means:

- Do not enter the pattern at your inside fix.
- From your inside fix, fly to the opposite fix, parallel to downwind but a mile or more outside it.
  - Chanhassen <--> Interchange
  - Elevator <--> Bridge
- Tower will point out the traffic to follow and maneuver you to join the pattern behind them.

For example:
- Landing RWY 28: instead of entering at Chanhassen, continue eastbound toward Interchange.
- Landing RWY 10: instead of entering at Bridge, continue westbound toward Elevator.

### Example Radio Calls

**Arriving from the west, landing RWY 28:**

> *"FCM Tower, N123, inbound High School with Alpha."* (on 125.2)

Possible tower responses:

| Response                                                 | What to do                                                                      |
| -------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| *"N123, roger."*                                         | Continue to Chanhassen. Report over Chanhassen if no further instructions.      |
| *"N123, after Chanhassen enter right downwind RWY 28R."* | Fly over Chanhassen, then enter right downwind.                                 |
| *"N123, enter right downwind RWY 28R."*                  | Enter the standard right traffic pattern normally.                              |
| *"N123, remain outside the downwind."*                   | Fly eastbound from Chanhassen toward Interchange. Tower will call your traffic. |

**Arriving from the southeast, landing RWY 10:**

> *"FCM Tower, N123, over Soccer Dome with Alpha."* (on 119.15)

Possible tower responses:

| Response                                             | What to do                                                               |
| ---------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| *"N123, roger."*                                     | Continue to Bridge. Report over Bridge if no further instructions.       |
| *"N123, after Bridge enter right downwind RWY 10R."* | Fly over Bridge, then enter right downwind.                              |
| *"N123, enter right downwind RWY 10R."*              | Enter standard right downwind normally.                                  |
| *"N123, remain outside the downwind."*               | Fly westbound from Bridge toward Elevator. Tower will call your traffic. |
